About Me

There is something about getting things “just right” that keeps me going. Whether it's a perfectly crafted sentence or a meticulously planned project, I thrive on the satisfaction of knowing that I've given my all for my achievements.

As an investigative learner, I find myself constantly seeking new knowledge and insights, always curious about how things work and how I can improve my skills. Whether it's tinkering with websites, experimenting in the kitchen, or attempting a new language for the fiftieth time, I am always up for a challenge. I believe that life is about constantly growing and pushing yourself, and I try to live by that every day.

In my free time, I enjoy designing and building websites, going on outdoor adventures, and spending time with friends and family. Life is about balance and it is important to make time for the things that truly matter. I am also extremely passionate about Jesus. If you find me randomly breaking out into song, don't mind me—I'm only praising the goodness of God.

From high5 Strengths Test

  1. In a team environment, optimists are grateful for people and circumstances, generous with praise and quick to point out the positive side of every situation. This is key to motivating people and mitigating conflicts.

    • Marketing
