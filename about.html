02. Strengths

From High5 Strengths Test

Understanding and leveraging one's personal strengths in the workplace can lead to greater job satisfaction, improved performance, and more successful outcomes for both the individual and the organization. By focusing on their strengths, individuals are able to work more effectively and efficiently, and contribute more effectively to the success of their team and the company as a whole.

  1. 1

    Optimist

    In a team environment, optimists are grateful for people and circumstances, generous with praise and quick to point out the positive side of every situation. This is key to motivating people and mitigating conflicts.


    Well-suited for:

    • Leading a team
    • Sales
    • Project management
    • Marketing
  2. 2

    Empathizer

    When part of a team, this strength based on kindness is valuable for mitigating conflict. Empathisers use it to ensure that everyone takes the others' challenges and emotions into consideration.


    Well-suited for:

    • Human resources
    • Team management
    • Sales
    • Mediation
  3. 3

    Believer

    Believers' actions are driven by core values that cannot be compromised at the expense of success, and team members respect their integrity, consistency, and reliability.


    Well-suited for:

    • Human resources
    • Public relations
    • Ethical compliance
    • Quality control
  4. 4

    Thinker

    Thinker's digest complex information and ideas before communicating them with the outside world. They can simplify sophisticated concepts in a way that even children can understand.


    Well-suited for:

    • Market research
    • Strategy
    • Product development
    • Leading a team
  5. 5

    Peacekeeper

    Peacekeeper's are masters of balance. Their strength helps them find alignment and build bridges among people to reach the best outcomes.


    Well-suited for:

    • Human resources
    • Leading a team
    • Public relations
    • Conflict resolution

Business Plan

Created 2021

The purpose of this assignment was to create a hypothetical business plan for an existing company on the NASDAQ—or of our own creation. As a gaming fan and user of the company's products, I chose NVIDIA to build this project around. The plan was broken into four parts, all building on top of each other until the final project was due. The information within this business plan was procurred directly from NVIDIA's 10-K and Corporate Financial Responsibility reports, and their use is strictly theoretical.
